About

          
Ashutosh is currently pursuing a Ph.D. at the Centre for Sustainable Technologies, IISc Bengaluru. Prior to joining IISc, he engaged in research at the CSIR Advanced Material Processing and Research Institute (AMPRI) during his M.Tech dissertation, focusing on 3D concrete printing. Additionally, he worked as a Research Associate at the CSIR Central Building Research Institute (CBRI), Roorkee, supported by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India. He contributed to projects such as "Post Earthquake Reconstruction of Schools in Nepal," "Improving Disaster Resiliency of Lifeline Structures in Himachal Pradesh State," and "Agro-Industrial Alkali-Activated Composites for 3D Concrete Printing and Modular Volumetric Construction," funded by the Government of Himachal Pradesh and the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), respectively. His research interests include 3D concrete printing, low-carbon materials, and supplementary cementitious materials for additive construction.
